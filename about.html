I work as a freelance cameraman and photographer across a variety of genres.

I have worked on a wide range of productions, starting my career working on high end snowboarding and surfing documentaries and going on to create a documentary in Zambia, shooting for ITV3 and Yesterday, and I currently produce training content for Great Ormond Street Hospital.

Throughout this time I have made music videos and short films, striving to improve my skillset. I shoot as much as possible on DSLR, as I love the look, and it means I have to always be on my guard to get the best possible shots. DSLR is also great as it's lightweight and allows both video and photography through a single camera.
